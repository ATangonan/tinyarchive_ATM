About Us

Welcome to the archive of Beanie Baby Cards!

This archive is presented to you by Adam Tangonan, Taline Kevranian, and Magdalena Avila.
This archive is being shown and exhibited in order to pay homage to and remember the fad of the
beanie babies and where they come from. In a few years when a kid goes up into the attic and
finds their parent's cards, beanies, and memorabilia they will have no idea what these things are
and the impact they had on the world at the time. That is why we are here, to change that
narrative. Kids can use this archive to read about and engage with what their parents used to play
with when they were their age. All of these cards were donated by a small business in
Burlingame called Nuts for Candy which has been selling beanie babies and beanie baby cards
for over 20 years. We hope this brings you a little nostalgia and or insight into what kind of toys
and collectible cards kids were playing with in the 90s. Have fun, explore, and immerse
yourselves in all things beanie!
